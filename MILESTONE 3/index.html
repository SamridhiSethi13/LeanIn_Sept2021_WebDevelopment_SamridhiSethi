Our Services

Dine-In

The place provides hygienic quality of foods starting right from breakfast to dinner, at a pocket-friendly price range. The fame of the restaurant lies in its multi-cuisine, North Indian, Continental and Oriental food that it provides. Besides delivering excellent staff service, the food hub provides positive vibes as soon as you step into the restaurant. Teenagers mostly avail the outdoor seating facility.

Take-Away

We also offer take- away facility and also accept pre-orders.The menu is economically budgeted and serves delicious North Indian, Chinese and Continental that is worth the price. You can also order packed but fresh food in bulk for parties but for large quantity please order in advance. We also provide immediate take-aways on certain items.

Home Delivery

One of the must-visit hangout spots whenever you're in the city, Samridhi's Restaurant is true to both its name and stellar reputation. The place is known for its fabulous food and mocktails, which coupled with the laid-back ambience, make for an assured good time. But there isn't much to complain about even though the ambience might be off-limits for a while, because we will deliver your favourite picks right to your doorstep!
