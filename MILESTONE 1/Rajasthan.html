ITINERARY

  • Day 1: Arrival at Jaipur
    Pick up from Jaipur Airport, Railway Station by placard vcarrying driver and transfer to hotel. Later sightseeing tour of the city. Evening visit Chokhi Dhani for Rajasthani traditional Dinner with dance and Music. Overnight at the 3-star Umaid Bhawan hotel or similar.

  • Day 2: Jaipur - Shekhawati - Bikaner
    After breakfast drive towards Bikaner. Visit to Camel-breeding Farm just before Bikaner (open 3 - 5 pm). Overnight at the 3-star Maharaja Ganga Mahal hotel or similar.

  • Day 3: Bikaner - Jaisalmer
    Ater bfreakfast drive towards Jaisalmer. Reach Jaiselmer before sundown, to catch a glimpse of the setting sun on the Golden Fort. Overnight at the 3-star Fort Rajwada hotel or similar.

  • Day 4: Jaisalmer
    After breakfast full day sightseeing tour of the city. After lunch proceed to Kuldhera - an abandoned habitation, and then to Sam Sand Dunes. Take a Camel Ride and experience the haunting magic of the tale of Dhola Maru. Overnight at the 3-star Fort Rajwada hotel or similar.

  • Day 5: Jaisalmer - Jodhpur
    After early breakfast, leave for Jodhpur, Check-in at hotel in Jodhpur. Afternoon sightseeing tour of Jodhpur. Overnight at the 3-star Pal Haveli hotel or similar.

  • Day 6: Jodhpur - Mount Abu
    Leave after early breakfast for Mt. Abu via Ranakpur Temples. Check-in at hotel in Mt. Abu. Visit to Sunset Point in the evening. Overnight at the 3-star Jaipur House hotel or similar.

  • Day 7: Mount Abu - Udaipur
    Visit Dilwara Temples and Brahmakumari Ashrams after breakfast. Leave for Udaipur after lunch. Overnight at the 3-star Ram Pratap Palace hotel or similar.

  • Day 8: Udaipur
    Full day Sightseeing of Udaipur, covering the City Palace, Old City, Saheliyon ki Badi and Haldi Ghati. Overnight at the 3-star Ram Pratap Palace hotel or similar.

  • Day 9: Udaipur - Pushkar - Jaipur
    After breakfast drive towards Jaipur enroute Ajmer & Pushkar. Trip ends after lunch at Jaipur.
